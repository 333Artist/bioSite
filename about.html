About

Adrian is inspired by a variety of creative media and activites.

Background

Adrian was raised in Rosenberg, Texas by his mother - who is a hardworking photographer and who enjoys spending time with her sons.

As a Jehovah Witness, he values family life and community support, and outside this community he shows this with his welcoming attitude to others.

"I try to make time for myself, family, and friends."

He works part-time while pursing a career in video game music production.

"One of my greatest achievements was making my mom watch the whole Naruto anime series. Not every parent has the time to sit down and watch what their kids watch and it is a LONG series. Now she is a fan of anime and we sometimes go to anime conventions [together].".

"I have always loved music, games, watching shows, movies, drawing, I sometimes cosplay at anime conventions, and I like to read Marvel comics."

"A really good table game I like to share and play with is called Dice Throne. It is a fast turn based game packed with action and strategy thinking."

"What I enjoy most of my hobbies is creating something from imagination. That includes music and art. If I can think about something, I would like to express it with music and art."

Challenges

Adrian can find himself missing out on time with friends, working, and pursing his hobbies because of sporadic allergies.

"I struggle with severe allergies and a condition that makes allergies worse. I often have days that prevent me from doing anything I want to do so I try to push through it."
